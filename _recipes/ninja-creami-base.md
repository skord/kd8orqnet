# My Ninja Creami Base
As much as this sort of recipe blogspam bothers me, I find myself doing it. 
## Ingredients
Yields 650ml 
- **1 cup (240ml) whole milk**
- **1 cup (240ml) heavy cream**
- **1/2 cup (100g) sugar**
- **1 tsp vanilla extract**
- **1/16 tsp xanthan gum**
- **1/8 tsp guar gum**
- **1/8 tsp salt**
